# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want VibeChat to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project for people experimenting with developer tools, AI workflows, and open-source contributions.

Please be thoughtful, direct, and kind. Good-faith disagreement is fine. Personal attacks, harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or disruptive behavior are not.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.
- Focus feedback on behavior, code, docs, or product decisions.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Share examples and evidence when reporting bugs or concerns.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abuse
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith trolling
- Posting private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
